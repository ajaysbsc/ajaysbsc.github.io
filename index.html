Antarctica Scientific Expedition: 44th Indian Antarctic Expedition

During the 44th Indian Scientific Expedition to Antarctica, we deployed advanced UAV systems for high-resolution mapping of glacial features. Our team conducted comprehensive surveys integrating RTK GPS for centimeter-level accuracy, enabling detailed analysis of ice dynamics and crevasse detection in this critical region of Earth's cryosphere.
