Antarctica Scientific Expedition: 44th Indian Antarctic Expedition

During the 44th Indian Scientific Expedition to Antarctica, we deployed advanced UAV systems for high-resolution mapping of glacial features. Our team conducted comprehensive surveys integrating RTK GPS for centimeter-level accuracy, enabling detailed analysis of ice dynamics and crevasse detection in this critical region of Earth's cryosphere.

About Me

I am a doctoral researcher at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, focusing on Himalayan glacier dynamics in the context of climate change. My research integrates remote sensing techniques with UAV-based surveys to analyze glacier mass balance and assess supraglacial debris thickness.

I aim to improve the understanding of glacier-climate interactions and contribute to more accurate estimates of future water availability in high-mountain Asia.
